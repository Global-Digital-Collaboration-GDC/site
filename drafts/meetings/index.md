# Meetings

This section holds recurring organizer meeting information and minutes.

!!! tip "Meeting hygiene"
    Publish short notes fast. A lightweight summary posted on the same day is more useful than perfect minutes that arrive two weeks later.

## Suggested cadence

- Q1 kickoff and planning alignment
- regular virtual organizer calls
- pre-conference task force checkpoints
- post-decision follow-up as needed

## Meeting note template

Use this structure for new pages:

```markdown
# YYYY-MM-DD Organizer Meeting

## Attendees

- Name

## Agenda

- Topic

## Notes

- Decision or discussion summary

## Actions

- [ ] Action item — Owner — Due date
```
