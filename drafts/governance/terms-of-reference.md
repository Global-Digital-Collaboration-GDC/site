# Terms of Reference

## Status

Draft placeholder for organizer review.

## Purpose

The organizer group supports the planning and delivery of Global Digital Collaboration 2026 by coordinating agenda design, stakeholder engagement, operations, and thematic workstreams.

## Scope

The group may:

- define conference planning priorities
- establish task forces for time-bound topics
- review major milestones and dependencies
- maintain public-facing documentation where appropriate

The group does not replace the authority of the host organization, event producer, or sponsoring entities.

## Meetings

Unless otherwise agreed, organizers meet on a recurring cadence and may use additional asynchronous review between calls.

## Decisions

The default goal is consensus. When consensus is not practical, the group may document an explicit decision, note any objections, and record the responsible lead for follow-up.

## Publication

This site may publish summaries, reference documents, and task force updates that are suitable for public visibility.
